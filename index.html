FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your interior design journey.

How long does a full home interior project take?
For a standard 2-3 BHK apartment, our projects typically take 30-45 days from design approval to handover. We offer a 45-day move-in guarantee on all projects.
What is included in the project cost?
Our pricing is fully transparent and includes design consultation, 3D visualization, all materials, manufacturing, transportation, installation, and project management. No hidden charges.
Do you offer EMI or finance options?
Yes! We offer easy EMI options starting from 0% interest through our banking partners. You can pay in installments — typically 30% to start, 60% during execution, and 10% on handover.
What warranty do you provide?
We offer a lifetime warranty on all HiLook-manufactured products (modular kitchen, wardrobes, storage units) and a 1-year warranty on all civil work including painting, flooring, and false ceilings.
Can I make changes after the design is approved?
Yes, minor changes can be accommodated during the early execution phase. Major design changes may affect cost and timeline. We encourage thorough review during the design phase to minimise revisions.
Do you work outside major cities?
Currently we serve Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Delhi NCR, Pune, and Coimbatore. We're rapidly expanding — contact us if your city isn't listed, and we'll let you know when we're available.
